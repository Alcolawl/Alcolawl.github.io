Relay.


The culmination of skill, speed and strength. With alcohol.

PlayersDifficultyDrunk Scale
2+4/74/7
Materials Required & Game Setup
  • A table, 2 Pong Balls, beer and 10+ Cups.
  • This game plays out like a relay race. Two teams stand on the same side of the table in a single file line for each team.
  • A cup is placed in front of both teams 6 inches from the edge of the table. Then two cups are placed in the center of the table.
  • Then 3 cups are placed vertically at the end of the table (like a straight line in pong), again, one for each team. (Refer to the diagram above).
  • Make sure to fill each cup 1/3 to 1/2 the way with beer.
1.When the game begins, the first player on both teams must bounce a pong ball into the nearest cup.
2.Once they've accomplished that, they must chug the contents of the cup and then shoot the pong ball into the center cup.
3.If the player misses, a teammate must run and retrieve the ball, at this time, if they encounter a player on the other team they are allowed to shove, distract, trip, etc that player to slow their retrieval of the ball.
4.Once they've nailed the center cup, they must chug the contents of that cup and move on to the final phase of the game.
5.During the final phase, the player must shoot at the final 3 cups at the end of the table in hopes of sinking just ONE.
6.Once they've done so they must run to the end of the table and chug the cup they just made, flipping it afterwards, like one would in a game of flip cup. The flipped cup is then removed from the game.
7.Immediately afterward, the team must fill their cups again with beer so that the next player in line can go. The team that eliminates all of their cups at the end of the table first wins.
8.NOTE: no contact is allowed while chugging the final cups, only while you're enroute to that end of the table.
