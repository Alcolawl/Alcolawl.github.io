Baseball.

A Team-based pong game based on chugging stupid amounts of booze.

PlayersDifficultyDrunk Scale
6+3/75/7
Materials Required & Game Setup
  • 14 Solo Cups and at least 1 pong ball.
  • Split all players into two equal teams.
  • Line up 4 cups vertically at each end of the table, fill them with beer.
  • Line up 3 cups at both long sides of the table (6 cups total). These cups should be lined up parallel to the sides of the table. Each of these cups must be filled with beer. The cup on the left is filled with an inch of beer, the middle cup 1/2 way, the third cup 2/3.
1.Teams take turns "pitching" and "batting". The batting team throws the pong ball and tries to get it in one of the opponents cups. The pitching team essentially does nothing and just hopes the batters suck at life.
2.If you really suck and miss three times, you're out (three strikes).
3.Three outs ends a half inning and the teams switch roles.
4.If the batting team gets the cup closest to them, it's a single, the second cup is a double, the third a triple and the fourth a home run.
5.If a batter gets on base, they move to the side of the table and stand in front of the three differently filled cups. The pitching team then sends a player of their choice to stand in front of their three differently filled cups on the other side. The batter is now on a "runner" on base, and his teammate is now allowed to go. At any time, the runner is allowed to "steal". Depending on the cup that the runner scored previously, they start on the left, middle or right cup. The left signifies first base, the middle as second and right as third. If the player decides to steal, they must chug a cup ahead of their base (if you're on second, drink the third base cup). If the pitching team's chugger notices, they must chug their cup as well. If the picthing team's chugger finishes before the runner, they're out. If the chugger finishes before the pitcher's team, they have successfully stolen the base.
6.Team that has the highest score at the end of the agreed amount of innings wins.
