Kings².

PlayersDifficultyDrunk Scale
4+1/78/7

A hybrid of Death Square and Kings. Absolutely deadly.

1.The Dealer passes out a 4 x 4 grid of cards (16 total for you math geniuses) face down.
2.Each column of the grid has a different rule. The first column is Red or Black, where the player must guess if the card is Red or Black.
3.The second column is Higher or Lower, where the player flips the card and must guess if the card the dealer has to flip will be higher or lower than the card on the table.
4.The third column is Suit, where the player simply guesses the card's suit.
5.The fourth and final column is Exact Card, where the player guesses the exact card that they're about to flip.
6.Traverse the columns vertically. So you finish the first, second, third and fourth columns in order.
6.Each card that is flipped has the same function as it would in Kings. (Ex. 7 is Heaven. Last player to point upwards must drink.) The player keeps going until they guess a card incorrectly.
8.ADDITIONALLY. If they guess the card wrong, EVERY player in the game must drink for the card's value in seconds. Ace = 14, King = 13, Queen = 12, Jack = 11, Ten = 10, and so on. So it's possible to drink twice for each card.
9.The game ends when all cards have been flipped.
