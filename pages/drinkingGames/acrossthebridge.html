Across The Bridge.

PlayersDifficultyDrunk Scale
2+1/74/7

This game is simple, and short-lived fun, but great for pounding a few beers in a short amount of time.

1.Take 10 cards and line them up on the table, face down. This is "The Bridge"
2.The first player flips a card, starting at one end of the bridge, and keeps flipping each consecutive card until they flip a Face card or an Ace.
3.When a Face card or Ace is flipped, the player must drink for the amount that the card represents, and add the same amount of cards onto the bridge. For Example, if the player gets a king, the player must drink for 3 seconds add 3 cards to the bridge. 1 for Jack, 2 for Queen, 3 for King and 4 for Ace.
4.After a player is done adding the cards to the bridge, the next player may go. The goal of the game is to reach the end of the Bridge. The player that reaches the end wins or something.
