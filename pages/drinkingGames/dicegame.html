Thrice Dice.

Amount of Players: 2+
Difficulty: 1/7
Drunk Scale: 6/7

A rapid-fire game that literally requires 4 brain cells to play.
1.Grab 3 dice.
2.The first player begins the game by rolling the dice. The player keeps any dice that land as a 3, and passes the rest off to the next player.
3.The next player then rolls the dice. The previous player now must drink in seconds for the total that the dice add up to and subtract 3 seconds for any 3s that they rolled before hand.
4.The player that just rolled now collects all the dice once more and rolls for 3s and then passes off the dice to the next player. The cycle repeats.
