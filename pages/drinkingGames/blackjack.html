Blackjack.

A watered down version of casino Blackjack with one goal: Drink!

1.Dealer passes out one card face down to each player, then one card face up.
2.The goal of the game is to get your hand to equal 21, or get as close as possible without exceeding 21.
3.If you exceed 21, your hand is bust and you must drink for 5 seconds plus the difference of your hand minus 21.
4.Each number card is it's own value. A 3 of Hearts equals 3. All face cards equal 10, and Aces equal 11 or 1. What an ace equals is up to the player to decide.
5.The Dealer then goes clockwise in a circle to each player. Each player must indiciate whether they'd like another card (hit) or they'd like to stay. If a player stays, their turn is over.
6.Any players that undershoot 21 must drink the difference in seconds.
7.The person that get's 21 (blackjack) wins and is allowed to hand out 5 seconds of drinking time to whom ever they choose.
