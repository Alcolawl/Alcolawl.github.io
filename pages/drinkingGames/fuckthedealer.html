Fuck The Dealer.

Amount of Players: 3+
Difficulty: 1/7
Drunk Scale: 5/7

You don't wanna be the dealer, trust me.
1.This game is fairly simple. Your task is to guess the card in the dealer's hand. That's all.
2. The dealer shuffles the deck and then holds the entire deck in their hand, flips a card and proceeds to ask the player to their left what the value of the card is (No suit, just value. Ex. 7).
3. If the player is wrong on the first guess, the dealer states whether the person's first guess was higher than the card, or lower. The player gets one more guess. If they're wrong this time, they drink the difference between their second guess and the actual card's value.
4. If the player is correct on the first guess the dealer drinks 4 seconds. If the player is correct on the second guess, the dealer drinks for 2 seconds.
5. After each second guess, whether the player is right or wrong, the card is placed face up on the table so the other players can keep track of the cards that have already been played.
6.The dealer passes off the deck after 3 players have consecutively gotten all of their guesses wrong.
7. At first, it doesn't seem like the Dealer is at a disadvantage, but as the collection of cards on the table grows, so do the chances of the players guessing correctly.
