Beerio Kart.

Players: 2+
Difficulty: 2/7
Drunk Scale: 4/7

Our take on the popular frat house sport.
1.First of all, you need an N64 (or we guess a Game Cube if you like the new game) and at least one other player.
2.This game is meant to be played with beer. Grab a beer of your choice (Cup or Can recommended).
3.V.S. mode, host chooses difficulty, character and track of their choice.
4.The goal of the game is to finish your beer by the end of the three laps.
5.Do whatever you must do to finish your beer in the time allotted. You may NOT, however, drink your beer before the race starts; that's cheating.
6.You CAN drive, but it'll be difficult steering, so it's not always the best choice.
7.First person to cross the finish line AND finish their entire beer wins and gets to choose the next race.
8.If you cross the finish line without finishing your beer, you automatically lose because you can't follow directions.
