Rapid Fire Beer Pong

PlayersDifficultyDrunk Scale
6+2/7~5/7
Materials Required & Game Setup
  • A table or two, 6 Pong Balls, Beer and 18 Cups.
  • 3 people per side, 3 cups lined up vertically in front of each person.
  • Note:This game can be played with more than 6 players as long as it's even number. Just multiply the required materials accordingly.
1.The goal of the game is to drain all of your opponents' cups.
2.The game is rapid-fire, no turns. As soon as you have a pong ball in your hand, you can shoot.
3.If all of a player's 3 cups are made, they must stand at the center of the side of the table. Here, they can play as a disruptor. Meaning, if one of the pong balls lands on the floor they can kick or slap it around the room, as long as they keep one foot centered with the table.
4.The team that sinks all of their opponents cups wins.
