Beer Ball.

The culmination of pong and chugging skills in one short but brutal game.

Players: 4
Difficulty: 2/7
Drunk Scale: 4/7

Materials Required
4 fresh beers, a square table or pong table, pong ball and a shot glass for measuring.
1.Create two teams of two and stand at opposing ends of the table, each player places a fresh unopened beer on the table in front of them.
2.Decide who starts with the ball first. If you can't decide, stop being bitches and give it to the oldest player.
3.The goal of the game is to throw the ball and hit one of the opposing team's cans.
4.Players take alternating turns throwing the ball in the following order: Player 1 (Team A), Player 2 (Team B), Player 3 (Team A), Player 4 (Team B).
5. If you or your teammate are successful at hitting one of the opposing team's cans, your team may crack open your beers and start drinking. At this point the other team must retrieve the ball and place it on the side of the table. Once your team has finished their beers, you may submit your empty cans to the other team.
6.The other team must now empty the remaining foam and beer into the shot glass. If the foam and beer pass an agreed upon point in the shot glass, you must grab a fresh beer and the game continues.
7.If you successfully finish your beer without the leftover contents passing the agreed upon point, you are done drinking, but will still throw during your turn.
8.The team who's players both finish their beers successfully wins the game.
