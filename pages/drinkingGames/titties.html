Titties.

Backboarding pong balls off your partner's chest. Girls might have a slight advantage.

PlayersDifficultyDrunk Scale
43/74/7
Materials Required & Game Setup
  • 17 Solo Cups and at least 4 pong balls.
1.Set up: 2 people per team and a rectangular table (pong table works fine).
2.Place four cups in each corner of the table set up in a diamond, pour a full can of beer into each of the four diamonds.
3.Teammates stand diagonally from eachother (so your opponent is standing next to you).
4.Place a single cup in the middle of the table, fill with a moderate amount of beer.
5.The player that starts begins by counting off "3,2,1, Titties!"
6. The goal of the game is to bounce a pong ball off the table and have it backboard against your teammate's chest, and have the ball land in one of the four cups. Note that the teammate can maneuver any way they like to get the ball to hit their chest.
7.If the ball successfully goes in you pass the cup to the person next to you. They have to drink the cup, then successfully flip the cup (like in flip cup) before they can go again.
8.If one person on your team has made all of their cups they may keep passing the ball to their teammate until they are done too.
9.Once a team has finished their cups, you have the bounce the ball twice into the cup in the middle. Note the ball must bounce on the table twice.
10.First team to do this wins.
