Higher-Lower.

This is a "team" game with really simple rules. You will almost never win though, goodluck.

PlayersDifficultyDrunk Scale
3+1/76/7
1.Layout nine cards faceup in a 3 x 3 square on the table.
2.The game starts with a player choosing a card in the square and guessing whether or not the next card in the deck will be higher or lower than the card they chose on the table.
3.After the player guesses, you place the card from the deck over the card that they chose in the square. The cards pile up over time, so you're a victim of your own success.
4.If the player guessed correctly keep the card as it is and it is now the next player's turn. However, if the player guessed incorrectly you must count the amount of cards in the pile and each player at the table must now drink in seconds for that amount. (ex. 5 cards = 5 seconds). You then must flip that pile and you can no longer play from that pile.
5.Play until all of the piles are facedown or all of the cards are gone from the deck.
6.If you win, go crazy! It doesn't happen often.
7.If you lose blame the idiot who guessed higher on a Jack because "they like to live dangerously". Also, finish your drinks.
