Tourettes.

Amount of Players:3+
Difficulty: 1/7
Drunk Scale: 4/7

You don't know english.
1.The game is simple: The dealer plays against another player, one at a time.
2. The Dealer starts by flipping a card over. Quickly, both players must say a word that begins with the same letter that the card's value begins with. For example, the dealer flips a Two. So both players must say a word that begins with the letter "T".
3.The player that says a word first wins, but the word cannot be the number and must be correct and part of the English Language.
4.If the player says an incorrect word, they automatically lose.
5.If the dealer loses, they pass off the cards to another player. If a player loses, they must take a drink.
