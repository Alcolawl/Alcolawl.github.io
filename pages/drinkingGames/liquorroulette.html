Liquor Roulette.

A nerve racking game of intuition and blind luck.

PlayersDifficultyDrunk Scale
3+2/74/7
Materials Required & Game Setup
  • An even amount of identical shot glasses (at least 6), Water, and a Clear Liquor.
1.One player is desgnated the shot pourer. The shot pourer's job is to fill the majority of the shot glasses with water and only a few with liquor, while the other players look away.
2.The shot pourer then lines up the shot glasses in a row on the table.
3.Choosing the glasses at random, two players must take an equal amount of shots one after another.
4.Ultimately, some players can get all water shots, while others may get the liquor shots.
