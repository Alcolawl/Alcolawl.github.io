A Little About Us...

Founded and created by a group of friends who work all day and party all night. Sometimes, though, you just like to stay in and not worry about the ride home or how much money you don't have left to waste at the bar. Sometimes chilling with your friends is all you need. And as many great ideas come about, this was inspired in exactly that kind of situation.

What better of a way to spread fun and drunkenness to others without spending a dime? We've all played a drinking game to a TV show, sporting event or movie before. So why not have a place to go to where you can find all of your favorite movie drinking games in one spot? 100% genuine, thought up, founded, and created by the D2M team themselves.
So sit back, crack open a cold one, and enjoy.
