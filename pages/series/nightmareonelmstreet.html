The Nightmare On Elm Street Series.

Universal Rules

These rules apply to all four movies.
First Of All:
1.Pick a character and whenever they talk, you drink. If they die, finish your drink and pick a new character.
Take a Drink When:
2.Someone says "Fred/Freddy".
3.The front of 1428 Elm Street is shown.
Take a Gulp When:
4.Someone has a nightmare.
5.You see the creepy jump rope kids or hear their song.
6.Someone dies.

Nightmare On Elm Street

Take a Drink When:
1.Someone says "Nancy" or "Glenn".
2.You see Johnny Depp in a new scene (Because it's his first movie).
Take a HUGE Gulp For:
3.The bathtub scene.
Finish Your Drink:
4.When Nancy steals Freddy's hat.

Nightmare On Elm Street Part 2: Freddy's Revenge.
