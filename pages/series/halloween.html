Halloween 1,2,3,4,5 & 6.

Universal Rules

These rules apply to all six movies.
Take a Drink When:
1.The classic Halloween music comes on.
2.There's a scene with Dr. Loomis.
3.You see a pumpkin or Jack-O-Lantern.
Take a Nice Gulp For:
4.Someone dies.
5.A sex scene or nudity.

Halloween (1981)

Take a Drink When:
3.Michael stalks someone.
1.Someone mentions "Halloween".
Take 2 Drinks When:
2.There's a full shot of the Myers residence.
Salute drink When:
3.The dog dies.
Finish Your Drink When:
4.Michael Myers gets lit up (shot).

Halloween 2 (1981)

First off:
1.Take a huge gulp for Mr Sandman playing in the very beginning of the movie.
Take a Drink When:
2.You see a (waaaa)ambulance.
Finish Your Drink When:
3.Jimmy randomly passes out in the car.

Halloween 3: Season of the Witch (1982)

Take a Drink When:
1.Someone says "Ellie", "Chellis" or "Cochran".
2.The Silver Shamrock song plays.
3.You see a man in a suit.
4.Text on screen.
5.A classic horror movie is shown on one of the TVs in the movie.
Finish Your Drink When:
6.Ellie is captured.

Halloween 4: The Return of Michael Myers (1988)

Take a Drink When:
1.Someone says "Rachel" or "Jamie".
2.Someone screams or cries.
Finish Your Drink For:
3.The dead dog.
4.Michael getting lit up by a ton of guns at the end.

Halloween 5: The Revenge of Michael Myers (1989)

Take a Drink When:
1.Someone says "Jamie" "Tina" "Mike/Michael" "Max".
2.Someone screams or cries.
3.There is a dog barking.
Finish Your Drink For:
4.Rachel's death

Halloween 6: The Curse of Michael Myers (1995)

Take a Drink When:
1.Someone says "Michael" "Tommy" "Danny".
2.Someone screams or cries (including the baby).
Finish Your Drink For:
3.Jamie's death.
4.When "The Man In Black" is revealed.
