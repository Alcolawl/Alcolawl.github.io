Paranormal Activity 1,2,3 & 4.

Universal Rules

These rules apply to all four movies.
Take a Drink When:
1.There's text on the screen.
2.There's a night time scene.

Paranormal Activity.

Take a Drink When:
1.The names "Katie" or "Micah" is said.
2.The time speeds up.
Take A Gulp When:
3.Katie is possessed.
4.Another character is on screen other than the main characters.
5.The rumbling sound happens.
6.The word "demon" or any form of it is said or referred to.

Paranormal Activity 2.

Take a Drink When:
1.The names "Ali", "Hunter", "Daniel", "Kristi" and "Martine" are said.
2.Hunter cries.
Take A Gulp When:
3.The vacuum is out of the pool.
Chug For 5 Seconds When:
4.Katie is on screen.
Finish Your Drink When:
5.The dog is attacked.

Paranormal Activity 3.

Take a Drink When:
1.The names "Kristi", "Katie", "Julie", "Dennis" or "Toby" are said.
2.There's a screeching sound.
Take A Gulp When:
3.Kristi talks to Toby..

Paranormal Activity 4.

Take a Drink When:
1.Someone says the names "Wyatt", "Robby", "Toby", "Alex" or "Ben".
Take 2 Drinks When:
2.Robby appears outta nowhere or let's himself onto their property.
3.The mom Is in the kitchen (extra gulp if she's cooking).
Take A Gulp When:
4.Someone views a video on a laptop.
5.You see "Toby".
6.Someone's facetiming.
Finish Your Drink When:
7.Katie appears on their couch.
