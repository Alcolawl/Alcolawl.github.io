The Resident Evil Series.

Universal Rules

These rules apply to all three movies.
First Of All:
1.Pick a character and everytime they talk in a new scene you drink. And if your character dies finish your drink.
Take a Drink When:
2.A gun is fired.
Take 2 Drinks When:
3.Raccoon City or Umbrella Corp. are mentioned.
4.There's a flashback.
Take a Gulp When:
5.The T-Virus is mentioned.
6.There's a Zombie attack.

Resident Evil.

Take a Drink When:
1.There's a split screen of security footage and the infrared map on screen.
Take A Gulp When:
2.Someone curses.
3.The Hive is mentioned
4.Someone gets bit by a Zombie.
Finish Your Drink When:
5.When Rain (Michelle Rodriguez) turns into a zombie.
