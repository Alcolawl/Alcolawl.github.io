The Toy Story Trilogy.

Universal Rules

These rules apply to all three movies.
Take a Drink When:
1.A song comes on.
2.Woody or Buzz say a catch phrase.
3.The toys have to freeze because a human is approaching.
Take 2 Drinks When:
4.A new toy is introduced.
And If You're Not a Baby... (+2/7)
5.Drink whenever the word "toy" is said.
6.Pick a character and anytime they talk in a new scene, you drink!

Toy Story.

Take a Drink When:
1.The name "Buzz Lightyear" is said.
2.Whenever the toys insult eachother.
3.Buzz tries to reach Star Command.
Take 2 Drinks When:
4.Buzz tries to fly.
5.Buzz's "laser" is fired.
Take A Gulp When:
6.Sid makes his first appearance.
7.Sid's toys are all on screen at once.
Finish Your Drink When:
8.Woody and Buzz are back in Andy's possesion.

Toy Story 2.

Take a Drink When:
1."Andy" or "Cowboy" is said.
2.A cowboy phrase or reference is said.
Take 2 Drinks When:
3.Al's Toy Barn is mentioned.
4.The name "Buster" is said.
5."You saved our lives, we are eternally greatful." is said.
Chug for 5 Seconds When:
6.The first appearance of Emperor Zurg.
7.Woody realizes who he is.
Finish Your Drink When:
8.When all the toys make it home safe!
