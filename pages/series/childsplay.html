Child's Play 1,2,3 & 4

Universal Rules

These rules apply to all three movies.
Take a Drink When:
1.The names "Andy", "Chucky" or "Good Guy" are said.
Take a Huge Gulp When:
2.Someone dies.
Chug For 10 Seconds When:
3.There's a voodoo ritual.

Child's Play

Take a Drink When:
1.A Shot is fired.
Take a Gulp When:
2.You see the homeless guy that sells the Chucky doll.
Finish Your Drink When:
3.Chucky's face starts looking human.
4.You're startled or scared.

Child's Play 2

Take a Drink When:
1.The mentions the name "Kyle".
2.You see a clown.
Take A 2 Drinks For:
3.Someone goes on a swing.
Finish Your Drink For:
4.When Chucky gets thrown in the trash.
5.When Chucky explodes.

Child's Play 3

Take a Drink When:
1.The mentions the names "Barclay", "Shelton", "Charles", or "Tyler".
2.Chucky's face changes between looking human and plastic.
Finish Your Drink When:
3.Whitehurst sacrifices himself.

Bride of Chucky (Child's Play 4)

Take a Drink When:
1.The mentions the names "Tiffany", "Jesse" or "Jade".
2.A song plays.
3.There's very noticeable cleavage.
Finish Your Drink When:
4.Chucky proposes.
5.The adorable baby is born.
