MLB - Major League Baseball Game.


First of All:
1. First pick one of the teams playing, and a player on that team. You get one extra drink every time your player was involved in any play that leads to drinking. (If he touched the baseball at any point of the play).
Take a Drink When:
2.One of the announcers says "out" or "inning".
3.There's a full pitching count.
4.There's a broken bat.
5.Your team gets a single.
6.A player on the opposing team strikes out.
7.There's a 1-2-3 inning.
Take 2 Drinks When:
8.A player on your team hits a double.
9.Your team gets a double play (when fielding, not batting).
Take A Massive Gulp When:
10.A player on your team hits a triple.
11.A player on your team steals a base.
12.The announcers chat with a player or coach in the game.
Chug for 5 seconds:
13.For every Home Run hit.
Finish Your Drink When:
14.There's a Grand Slam or Triple Play.
