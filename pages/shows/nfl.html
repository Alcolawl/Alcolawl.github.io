NFL - National Football League Game.

First off:
1.Pick a team, if your team loses you finish a drink.
2.Also, every time a team scores, drink for the amount of points scored. (ex. 3 Points = 3 seconds).
Take a Drink When:
3.The ball is kicked.
4.There's a first down.
5.A coach is shown yelling.
Take 2 Drinks When:
6.A penalty is called.
Take A HUGE Gulp if:
7.The quarterback is sacked.
Chug for 5 Seconds for each:
8.Challenged play.
9.Interception.
10.Fumble.
Finish Your Drink If:
11.There is a safety.
12.A turnover is returned for a touchdown.
